About Me

Hi! My name is Pierre-Alexandre and I am currently a second year Master student in Computer Science at Northeastern University.

I was born and grew up in Brittany (France), less than 10 miles away from Carentoir, where Ubisoft was founded by Guillemot brothers in 1986.

I am currently working on:

  • Implementing a focused web crawler from from scratch (see details)
  • Back-end development for the User microservice of ClimateTree (see details)
  • Teaching a friend basic algorithms
  • Working part-time for the Information Technology department at my university
  • Responsive version of this website
