About Me

Hi! My name is Pierre-Alexandre and I am currently a second year M.S in Computer Science candidate at Northeastern University . I was born and grew up in Sixt-Sur-Aff, less than 10 miles away from Carentoir, where Ubisoft was founded by Guillemot brothers in 1986.

After few experiences in investment banking and three years as a reservist for the French Marines, I realized that I would like to leverage my love of technology in order to provide different impact on the society. I have broad interests in educational technology , on-demand economy, environmental technology and communication .
